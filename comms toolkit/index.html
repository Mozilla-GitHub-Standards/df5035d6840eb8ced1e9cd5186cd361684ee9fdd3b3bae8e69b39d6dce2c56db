MoFo Comms and Design Toolkit

Welcome to the Mozilla Foundation's Communication and Design Toolkit.

This toolkit has been developed to help Mozilla staff develop materials that are consistent and engaging.

Questions to consider when crafting communications:

  • What is the goal of your activity?
  • Who is the audience? What about this work appeals to this audience in particular?
  • What are the best channels to reach your audience?
  • How does this activity support the overall strategy?

Essential steps:

  • Make sure to fill out the communications support form at least 2 weeks before your deadline
  • Fill out a design brief if necessary
  • Make sure you are following Mozilla brand guidelines and the Mozilla styleguide
